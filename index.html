Who We Are

TCFoundations

TCFabrications was started from a love of metal work, helping people and a bit of good ole elbow grease. Starting fresh in a new city from 2018 the Melbourne pandemic and subsequent restrictions caused many a metal work shop to shutter. work was still in high demand so TCF was founded. A quick turn around while working with clients to maintain standards and government rergulations with a consitent quality result during the heights of the Melbourne pandemic saw TCF move into a new office and workshop in 2022 to meet the ever growing demand of quality metal craftsman ship expected from TCFabrication.
